About

Hi, I'm Davis Helms,

I'm a recent graduate from the New Media program at UNCA. I've just finished their animation track but I've taken classes in all branches.

I'm also a confident coder in many different programming languages with a minor in Computer Science.

I spend a lot of my free time experimenting in Unreal and Unity creating video games. Nothing published (yet).
